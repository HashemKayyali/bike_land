What is Bike Land?

Bike Land is Jordan’s interactive cycling show. We turn pedaling into a live experience: LED-powered racing, real-time stats on big screens, and brandable moments for malls, schools, festivals, and corporate events.

With Bike Land you can run pedal-powered games, the bike blender smoothie show, and competitive challenges that attract crowds and prove strong brand presence at big events.

Why brands book Bike Land

  • High-visibility LED visuals and live stats that pull a crowd.
  • Portable, quick setup across Jordan (malls, schools, festivals).
  • Full-brand customization: wheels, cups, roll-ups, and backdrops.
