What is Bike Land?

Bike Land delivers bike-based shows and games for events, companies, schools, and universities across Jordan. Our headline attraction is the Bike Blender: guests ride the bike and watch the blender spin to make fresh smoothies—served in brand-custom cups.

We also run bike challenge games (distance races, time trials, head-to-head duels) with portable setups, quick installation, and safety-trained staff—ideal for mall activations, orientation days, sports weeks, and corporate campaigns.

Why brands book Bike Land

  • Bike Blender smoothie show with cups customized to your brand.
  • Bike-based challenge games with live engagement.
  • Portable setup, quick install, safety-trained staff.
