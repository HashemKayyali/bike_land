Bike Beam

Competitive

A thrilling two–player race of speed and endurance! Each rider powers up their LED beam, climbing higher with every push. The challenge is set over a fixed distance — the first to reach the top wins. With three difficulty levels (Easy – Medium – Hard), it’s designed to match every participant and maximize excitement. Perfect for events, exhibitions, and activations that want strong competition and crowd-engaging fun.
